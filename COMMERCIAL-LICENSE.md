See [LICENSE](./LICENSE) for the full AGPL text.

A **commercial licence** is available for organisations that:

- Embed or redistribute Zaria as part of a product or service **without** open-sourcing their modifications under AGPL, **or**
- Require **Enterprise Edition** features (fleet auditing, SSO/SAML, RBAC, centralised dashboards, policy-as-code, priority support, Zaria Cloud, and more).

## How to Obtain a Commercial Licence

Contact us at **enterprise@zaria.dev** with:

1. Your organisation name and primary use case.
2. Approximate number of repositories or engineers that will use Zaria.
3. Whether you need self-hosted (on-premises), cloud-hosted (Zaria Cloud), or both.

We will respond within one business day.

## What the Commercial Licence Grants

- **AGPL-exemption:** Deploy and modify Zaria without triggering AGPL's source-disclosure obligations.
- **Enterprise features:** Access to fleet auditing, SAML/SSO, RBAC, audit history, policy-as-code, PDF reports, REST API export, and more (see [docs/open-source-strategy.md](./docs/open-source-strategy.md) for the full feature matrix).
- **Priority support SLA:** Guaranteed response times and a dedicated engineering contact.
- **On-premises agent:** Run Zaria fully air-gapped behind your firewall.

## What Remains Free Under AGPL

All features listed in the **AGPL Community Edition** section of [docs/open-source-strategy.md](./docs/open-source-strategy.md) are permanently free for AGPL-compliant use. These features will never be moved behind a commercial licence.
